## A clear and independent view of your electric vehicle

Naubat is an independent tool that helps you **understand how your electric or plug-in hybrid vehicle actually behaves** based on real usage data.

Our goal is to provide an **objective, clear, and useful** view of key topics such as:
- Daily vehicle usage
- The impact of your driving on the battery
- Battery health over time

Without relying only on generic manufacturer estimates.

---

## Who is Naubat for?

Naubat is built for:
- Electric vehicle users who want to **make decisions based on data**
- People who want to **avoid battery surprises** in the medium and long term
- Drivers who value **transparency and independence** in vehicle information

---

## A real data-driven approach

Naubat collects data directly from the vehicle during normal use and analyzes it to generate clear, actionable metrics.

You do not need technical knowledge or special tests:  
the app works in the background while you drive.
