# Privacy and Independence

Naubat is designed to provide independent, structured access to battery-related diagnostic data. The system is multi-brand by design, and both independence and transparency are core operating principles.

## Direct Diagnostic Access

Naubat communicates directly with the vehicle through standardized automotive diagnostic protocols.
The primary protocol used is UDS, Unified Diagnostic Services.
This is the same diagnostic layer used in professional workshops and during vehicle servicing.
No manufacturer cloud APIs are used.
No manufacturer telematics services are required.

Direct access to the diagnostic bus enables structured, high-fidelity battery signals.
This approach avoids dependency on manufacturer-specific app ecosystems.
The objective is not to replace manufacturer tools, but to provide an additional technically grounded perspective.

## Multi-Brand Philosophy

Naubat is not affiliated with any vehicle manufacturer.
The system is designed to operate across brands.
Vehicle support is prioritized according to user demand and technical feasibility.
Compatibility depends on reliable access to required battery signals.

The objective is to provide a second technical layer of insight.
Battery information that is often not exposed to the driver can be structured and analyzed.
The approach remains brand-agnostic.

## Battery Expertise

The analytical approach builds on battery analysis methodologies used in other energy storage domains.
Techniques commonly used in stationary storage systems and industrial battery analysis are adapted for automotive use.
Combining professional vehicle diagnostics with battery-domain expertise enables deeper technical insight.

## Data Privacy

Only the user's email is collected for authentication and communication purposes.
No VIN is stored.
No personal driving identity profile is created.
No vehicle manufacturer account is required.
No manufacturer cloud connection is required.

## Read-Only Operation

Naubat performs read-only diagnostic requests.
No parameters are modified.
No configuration, coding, or firmware flashing is performed.
Vehicle behavior is not altered.

This operating model is designed to be safe and non-intrusive.

## Why Independence Matters

Direct diagnostic access enables transparent battery analysis. A brand-independent system provides consistent methodology across vehicles. Independence supports objective interpretation of battery-related signals.
