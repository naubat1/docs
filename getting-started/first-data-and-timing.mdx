# First Data and Timing

After installation and initial setup, Naubat begins collecting data when the vehicle and dongle are available under normal operating conditions. This page explains expected sampling behavior and when information should appear.

## Sampling Frequency

Key battery signals are sampled at approximately 1 Hz.
Other parameters are sampled at approximately 60-second intervals.
Sampling frequency may vary by vehicle model and signal availability.

## Data Integrity

Occasional gaps may occur during operation.
Communication quality between phone, dongle, and vehicle affects sampling continuity.
Collected data is checked for quality before metrics are presented.

## When Will I See Data?

Dashboard updates are delivered in batches.
Under normal conditions, updates appear approximately every minute.
Vehicle activity is required for collection and metric generation.

## What If No Data Appears?

- Check that Bluetooth is enabled.
- Check that the app has not been manually closed.
- Check that required background permissions are enabled.
- Check that the dongle is compatible.
